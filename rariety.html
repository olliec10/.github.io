Character Rariety

In Monster legends there is something called Character Rariety. Character Rariety is the system that tells you how good and how hard it is to find that character. There are 5 different groups of Character Rariety and they are common, uncommon, rare, epic and legendary. These different groups define how good the character is.

Common

The Common group is the easiest group to breed and there are only 9 commons. To begin with this character group is useful but then becomes weak when new character groups are unlocked. This group is also used in breeding a lot.

Uncommon

The Uncommon group is the group you unlock when you breed two commons together and you unlock it. When you unlock this group it is a real improvement to your last team and will be helpful in your teams to come.

Rare

The Rare group is the next best group after the uncommon group. This group is not very useful at fighting but its main purpurse is to breed with other monsters from there group to breed epic monsters.

Epic

The Epic group is the group you mainly use in your team untill you unock the legendary group. The epic group is used to breed the next highest group. This group is also a group that is used in battle and is helpful at a high level.

Legendary

The Legendary group is a group that is the best group in the game and is used in battle alot. This group focuses on beating there oponunent and they definrntly do a good job at it.
