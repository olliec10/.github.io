Character Rariety

In Monster legends there is something called Character Rariety. Character Rariety is the system that tells you how good and how hard it is to find that character. There are 5 different goroups of Character Rariety and they are common, uncommon, rare, epic and legendary. These different groups define how good the character is.

Common

The common element is the easiest element to breed and there are only 9 commons. To begin with this character group is useful but then becomes weak when new character groups are unlocked. This group is also used in breeding alot.
