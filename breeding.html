Breeding

Breeding is when you unlock the breeding tree. You select two characters you already own and then press breed. You will see a time at the bottom of the screen and based on that time is the character you get. Once you breed and unlock all the characters you can get rewards by clicking on your monstagram then clicking on the icon in the top left corner you can check your progress

Common Breeding

When you breed two common monsters together you get an uncommon monster. Depending on which two element you breed together you get a diffferent monster..

Uncommon Breeding

When breeding with uncommon monsters you get rare monsters. When breeding you don't allways get rare monsters. Depending on wich elements you breed together you get a rare monster.

Rare

When Breeding with the rare monster you unlock the epic monster. There is not always a chance you will get an epic monster but when you do they are helpful in your team and are useful in breeding.

Epic Breeding

Breedig with epic monsters is different to the other ways of breeding with other characters. There are only a few known combinations to breed a few monsters and only try breeding legendary monsters when you have a consistant amount of epic monsters.

Legendary Breeding

When you breed with legendary monsters you can can only get common, uncommon and epic monsters because the legendary group is the highest group in the game.
