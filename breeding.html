Breeding

Breeding is when you unlock the breeding tree. You select two characters you already own and then press breed. You will see a time at the bottom of the screen the time and based on that time is the character you get. Once you breed and unlock all the characters you can get rewards by clicking on your monstagram then clicking on the icon in the top left corner you can check your progress

Common Breeding

When you breed two common monsters together you get an uncommon monster. Depending on which two element you breed together you get a diffferent monster..

Uncommon Breeding

When breeding with uncommon monsters you get epic monsters. When breeding you don't allways get epic monsters. Depending on wich elements you breed together you get an epic monster.
